Jewish Orange Strudel

  • Time: 1 1/2 hour preparing - 30 minutes baking
  • Serves: 10 people

Ingredients

Dough

Ingredients Quantity
All-Purpose Flour 1 1/4 cups
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Vegetable Oil 1/4 cup
Water 1/3 cups

Filling

Ingredients Quantity
Raisins 3/4 cups
Orange Peels 5 large oranges
Lemon 1 whole
Cinamon 2 tablespoons
Sugar 3 tablespoons
Berries from Jam 3 tablespoons

Preparation

Dough

Step 1: Combine the flour and salt in a medium bowl.
Step 2:Add the oil and water and mix with your hands until a rough dough forms.
Step 3:Turn it out onto a lightly floured counter and knead for 10 minutes, until soft and silky. Step 4:Form it into a ball, place it on the counter and cover withtowel. Let rest for 30 minutes.

Filling

Step 5:In a large bowl, peel all oranges and soak the peels in water.
Step 6:Zest lemon and blend with sugar and Cinamon
Step 7:Pick out whole berries in jam and keep as little liquid as possible
Step 8:Soak raisins in water in a bowl

Assembling

Step 9:After 30 minutes passed on the dough, start rolling out the dough. Roll out dough into an exlipse shape. The dough needs to be extremely thin, almost see through. If it rips, reform into a ball and restart. Leave the edges a little thicker
Step 10:After dough is rolled out, start assembling filling to go in lines going throughout the long side.
Step 11:Dry the raisins
Step 12:Dry the peels and cut them into tiny sqaures as small as possible
Step 13:In lines going alond the long side of the thin dough, start putting each filling ingredient in small thin lines
Step 14:Roll the dough slowly to making a long lon
Step 15:Cover a baking tray with butter and carefully place the roll without ripping
Step 16:Make thin scoring lines across the top for easier cutting after done baking Optional Step 17: If there is extra sugar or cinnamon, sprinkle on top

Baking

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
Step 2:Set the oven to 350 and place the baking tray for 30 minutes
Step 3:Cut along pre-cut lines and enjoy!
